ABOUT US

IMCO OFFICE PRODUCTS INTERNATIONAL PVT LTD, Chennai, INDIA is engaged in manufacture of writing instruments consisting of Ball Pens, Gels Ink Pens & Fine liner Pens. The Company has its manufacturing base at Chennai fully equipped with high end injection Moulding Machine, Automatic Refill Assembly Machine from Germany, Extrusion line and Multi Cavity Moulds imported from japan and Korea. The Company has the capacity to manufacture over 300 Million pieces per annum. The Company started its operation in 1995 and is currently exporting its products on to japan, Russia and to select countries in Europe and Middle East. It can undertake contract manufacturing for large requirements of reputed companies. The company has put in place the international standards including JIS and DIN standards to meet specific market requirements and the products carry shelf life assurance of two years from the date of manufacture. The company is managed by professionals having experience of over 26 years in the field of writing instruments. The IMCO proposition to customer is to provide high Quality value for money products at affordable prices with constant updating of technology meeting the changing requirements of the market. The Company is managed by Professionals having experience of over 26 years in the field of writing instruments. The CEO, Mr.G.Chandrasekar, a Chartered Accountant by profession, has over two decades of hands on experience in the writing instruments industry and is assisted by highly qualified and experienced team in all the functional departments including Design, Development, Manufacture, Marketing and Quality Assurance.
